Variation within structure

Running workouts are built on the training principles of Jack Daniels and Steve Magness.

Workouts scale based on your goal event. Shorter events emphasize faster reps and speed work; longer events emphasize threshold and sustained efforts.

Easy days: Add 6-8 strides (15-20 sec light, quick runs with 45-60 sec recovery) to 2+ easy runs per week.

Recovery: Respect the prescribed rest — it's part of the workout. The rest ensures quality reps and proper energy system training.

Strength workouts are designed for efficient training with whatever equipment you have.

The philosophy centers on compound movements and major chain training: upper pull, upper push, lower squat patterns, and lower hinge patterns. Each workout covers the relevant chains for balanced development.

Inspired by the creator's love of calisthenics and the principle that simple, consistent work on fundamental movements beats complicated programming.
